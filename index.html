Before NovaLayer, moving money between blockchains was a headache. You had to trust slow bridges, pay hidden fees, and wait hours for your own money.

Security Risks

Bridges are the #1 target for hackers. When you "wrap" a token, you're giving up control of your real assets to a third party.

Extreme Delays

Some transfers take 10 minutes; others take 7 days. In a fast market, waiting for a bridge means missing the opportunity.

Wasted Fees
